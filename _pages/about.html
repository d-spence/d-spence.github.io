About Me

Thanks for stopping by! My name's Dan.

I'm a determined, focused individual with a passion for learning and computer programming. While my primary interest is web-development, I also enjoy studying the broader concepts of software engineering and computer science. My current ambition is to work as a full-stack web developer and continue to refine and enhance my skillset.


This website serves as my personal portfolio with links and descriptions of the various projects I've worked on. If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to head over to the contact page and send me a message.


Background

From an early age, computers have fascinated me. Initially through PC games, later with software and operating systems and eventually with programming. In 2012 I started coding with Python, and continued to learn over the years. In 2020 I decided to focus my efforts on learning full-stack web development. My experience with Python has been a great help in the transition to JavaScript and allows a little more flexibility with the frameworks I use.
